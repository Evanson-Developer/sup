Membership Preparation

Whether you are Very Experienced, Somewhat Experienced or a Beginner, we can certify you as a Speed Pass Member.

Very Experienced Paddlers

Very Experienced Paddlers just rent one time at the $25 rate. After you are done paddling and have demonstrated safe control, the instructor will issue you a "skills sign-off" certification card (if you make the request when you sign up).

The next time you rent, if you bring the sign off card, a completed registration form and are prepared to apply for Membership, we will only charge you the membership rate of $16 plus the one-time Membership Fee! (See *Please Note)

Beginner or Novice Paddlers

Beginner or Novice Paddlers can take advantage of our free lessons included within the $25 rental rate. The instructor will give pointers on land on our buoy system before we get on the water. There is no charge for the training, and a guide will stay within range until both you and the instructor are in agreement that you are safe and confident to paddle on your own. Beginning paddlers can and should request the instructor to remain with them if they feel the need.

Most beginners become a Speed Pass Member after a single session. We reserve the right to require additional training. The paddler may also request as many lessons with a guide as they like before applying for membership. After you have demonstrated safe control, the instructor will issue you a "skills sign-off" certification card (if you make the request when you sign up).

The next time you rent, if you bring the sign off card, a completed registration form and are prepared to apply for Membership, we will only charge you the membership rate of $16 plus the one-time Membership Fee! (See *Please Note)
