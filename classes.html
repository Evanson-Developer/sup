Level One

We consider the Stand Up Membership a level one rating.

Level Two

Prerequisite - Stand Up Membership (or equivalent by our evaluation) - This class is for those who would like to advance their skills. The students will practice and receive training and evaluation on turning techniques, learn how to handle limited boat wakes, wind & chop, limited current & tide changes, advance falls, board retrieval, get introduced to race boards with a sea trial, learn problem recognition and prevention, introduction to rescue techniques. Members who pass this class (or those rated as equivalently experienced by us) may participate on a board on the Ultimate Dolphin Adventure!
