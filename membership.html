Speed Pass Membership

Speed Pass Members are guaranteed to get a quality board - with no wait! Enrollment is only a one-time $10 fee with no renewal costs and no expiration. Speed Pass Members paddle at $16 per hour, and only $10 per hour when launching before 9:15am. (Level Two Members at $12 per hour and $10 Early Bird)

Complete and print the online registration form and bring it back with your "skills sign-off" card. The Registration Form contains a written quiz and a permanent Waiver of Liability. Most of the answers are in Skills and Rules Of The Road. One of our staff will finalize your permanent membership with a quick Final Check Out.



Speed Pass Registration Form


*Please note: On your return visit if you have not been previously signed off, and/or you do not have a completed Registration Form, the rate is $25 per hour. Paddlers are charged the $25 rate until they are actively registering as a Speed Pass Member. The $10 per hour Early Bird Special is not applicable at the time of application.

Level Two Members paddle at $12 per hour, and are afforded the $10 early bird special Advanced Training & Level Two.
