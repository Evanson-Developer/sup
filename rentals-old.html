#2 Membership Privileges

Members may pay after they are done; this way may decide while they are out how long they wish to paddle.

Additional time over the first hour is pro-rated according to the rate and according to the minutes used. As an example, if a Speed Pass Member rents at the early bird rate at $10 per hour and paddles for an hour and 6 minutes, they would not be charged for the second hour, but only pay for the additional 6 minutes or $1 more, totaling an $11 charge for the one hour and 6 minutes. If the Early Bird paddles for 3 hours they only pay $30. Non-members additional time is prorated at $20 per hour.
